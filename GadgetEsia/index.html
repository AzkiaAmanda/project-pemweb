About Us

Samsung Electronics, founded in 1969 and headquartered in Suwon, South Korea, is a world-renowned leader in the technology industry. The company specializes in a wide range of products, including smartphones, semiconductors, consumer electronics, and home appliances, with a strong reputation for innovation and quality.

Best known for its Galaxy smartphones, QLED TVs, and advanced semiconductor solutions, Samsung Electronics plays a vital role in shaping the future of technology. With a focus on research and development, the company consistently delivers cutting-edge solutions to meet the evolving needs of consumers and industries worldwide.

Operating in over 80 countries, Samsung Electronics is committed to sustainability, innovation, and creating technologies that improve lives globally.
